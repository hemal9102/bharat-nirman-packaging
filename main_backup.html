About Us

At Bharat Neerman Packaging, we help liaison between packaging material manufacturers from India and International buyers. We provide access to reliable and cost-effective Packaging material manufacturers. We cater to both Indian as well as International clients. Our range of packaging solutions includes all kind of PP Woven bags, Corrugated Boxes, Flexible Laminates, Stretch films etc. We work as First level purchase managers for buyers and the core Marketing team for Indian Manufacturers. Creating value for both International Buyers and Indian Manufacturers with help of our extensive experience in packaging domain.
